Pokemon Memory Game

Instructions:

  1. Start the Game: Click "Start" to begin the game.
  2. Flip Cards: Click on two cards to flip them over.
  3. Match Pairs: Find matching pairs of cards.
  4. Try Again: If cards don't match, they'll flip back over after a short delay.
  5. Win: Complete all matches within the alloted time to win the game.
